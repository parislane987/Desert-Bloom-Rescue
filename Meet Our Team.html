Meet Our Team

Mark, General Veterinarian

Mark is our dedicated veterinarian with over 10 years of experience caring for animals of all shapes and sizes. Passionate about rescue work, he ensures every animal at Desert Bloom Rescue gets top-notch medical care and plenty of love.

Jordan, Zoologist

Jordan is our expert zoologist with a lifelong passion for animal behavior and conservation. With a deep understanding of both domestic and wild species, she helps create safe, enriching environments for all our animals.

Lillianna, Orinthologist

Lillianna is our dedicated orinthologist with a lifelong passion for birds of all types. Shee specializes in care, rehabilitation, and enrichment of our feathered friends, making sure every bird at Desert Bloom Rescue thrives in a safe and nurturing environment.

Fred, Herpetologist

Fred is our skilled herpetologist, bringing years of hands-on experience working with reptiles and amphibians. He focuses on rehabilitating injured wildlife and educating the community about the importance of conservation.

Adrian, Primatologist

Adrian is our expert primatologist, specializing in the care and study of primates. With a focus on physical health and social enrichment, Adrian ensures that any primates at Desert Bloom Rescue receive compassionate care tailored to their unique needs.
