Welcome to Desert Bloom Rescue!

Who We Are

Desert Bloom Rescue is a dedicated, community-driven organization based in the heart of New Mexico. We are committed to the rescue and rehabilitation of exotic animals in need. Founded out of a passion for wildlife preservation and animal welfare, Desert Bloom serves as a sanctuary for a wide variety of exotic species, including reptiles, birds, amphibians, and some small mammals. We at Desert Bloom recognize the challenges posed by exotic animal ownership – such as lack of proper care, knowledge, or behavioral issues – and we strive to provide a safe environment where these animals can receive the specialized attention they need. Our facility is equipped with habitats and staffed by trained caretakers, veterinarians, and dedicated volunteers who understand the needs of exotic creatures. Beyond rescue operations, Desert Bloom places an emphasis on education and advocacy. We aim to inform the public about endangered species, the importance of conservation efforts, and the dangers of illegal wildlife trade. We firmly believe that fostering a deep understanding of animals and the natural world can reduce the number of animals that end up in unsafe situations. Our New Mexico roots deeply influence our approach to animal care. We have adapted our habitats to provide accommodating environments for each unique animal. The arid desert climate presents opportunities for exotic animal welfare, and the beautiful creatures that inhabit our landscape can find a caring home within our facilities. Through compassion, expertise, and collaboration, Desert Bloom Rescue strives to be a beacon of hope for exotic animals throughout New Mexico – and beyond. (Learn more on our About Page).

Our Mission

At Desert Bloom Rescue, our mission is to provide compassionate, expert care to displaced, sick/injured, or surrendered exotic animals while promoting conservation and education. We are committed to rescuing and rehabilitating exotic species, ensuring their well-being, as well as raising awareness about the unique needs of exotic animals. We strive to work toward a future where every creature is treated with dignity and respect.
